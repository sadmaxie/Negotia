About Negotia

Negotia is a simple web-based tool designed to streamline the negotiation process between two parties. It allows each party to input their acceptable offer ranges and provides a smart, data-based final suggestion to help reach a mutually beneficial agreement.

  • Instant Calculations: Automatically computes averages and final fair value suggestions.
  • Currency Selection: Choose from a list of global currencies for clarity and context.
  • Private & Local: No server-side storage — all session data is saved temporarily in your browser.
  • Free & Open: This tool is open to all and maintained by the community.
